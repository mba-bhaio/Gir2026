Gir Jungle Safari — How to fill the booking form

Clear, step-by-step guidance to complete your Gypsy registration (Friday 16th January — Evening 3–6 PM)

Quick steps (do these in order)

  1. Form a group — Make a group of 6 or 8 people before you start the booking on the website.
  2. Open the booking page — Go to girlion.gujarat.gov.in → Gir Jungle Trail Booking. Choose Booking → Gir Jungal safari.
  3. Select date & time — Choose Friday 16th January and the slot Evening 3 to 6 PM. Confirm the date on the calendar carefully; bookings are non-refundable.
  4. Choose Gypsy seat — Select gypsy seat 4 / 6 / 8 according to your group size.
  5. Prepare ID proof images — Keep a soft copy for each member (Aadhar / Driving Licence / Election Card / Passport). Upload only .jpg, .jpeg, or .png and ensure each file is less than 3 MB.
  6. Fill details & pay token — Enter details for every member and pay the token amount online to confirm the registration.
  7. Confirm & share — Once booked, update your group chat with the registration confirmation. The booking is non-refundable, not transferable, and cancellations are not allowed.

Important notes

- Only confirmed online registered persons can travel in that Gypsy group. Exchanges not allowed — if someone is absent, the seat remains empty.
- No spot registration. For NRI/foreigners the amount may be higher. Camera carries additional cost. Phones are allowed. Plastic bottles are not permitted.

Checklist before you start

  • Group size Group of 6 or 8 ready
  • IDs Soft copies (.jpg/.jpeg/.png) — each < 3 MB
  • Payment Card/UPI/Netbanking ready for token amount
  • Date/Slot Friday 16th January — Evening 3 to 6 PM
  • Seat type Gypsy 4 / 6 / 8
